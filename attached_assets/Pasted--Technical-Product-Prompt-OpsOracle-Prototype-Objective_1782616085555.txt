# Technical Product Prompt – OpsOracle Prototype

## Objective

Build a modern enterprise-grade prototype called **OpsOracle**, an AI-powered Slack Incident Intelligence Agent.

The prototype should demonstrate the complete end-to-end workflow of detecting operational incidents, reasoning over historical context, discovering experts, creating incident war rooms, generating runbooks, and orchestrating incident response entirely inside a Slack-inspired interface.

This is a prototype, but it should look and behave like a production SaaS product suitable for a hackathon demonstration.
